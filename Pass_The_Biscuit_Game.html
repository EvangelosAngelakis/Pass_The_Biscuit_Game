Pass The Biscuit Game

1. Add all team names in the team name boxes..

2. Select one or all of the Biscuits from the drop down list.

3. Press the Biscuit button to select a word.

4. Start the timer. You have 1 minute in total.

5. Mime or explain the word to your teammate.

6. If your teammate guesses correctly, add a biscuit / point.

7. Follow steps 3 to 6 until the time runs out.

8. The Team with the most points wins the game.
